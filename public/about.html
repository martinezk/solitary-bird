ABOUT SOLITARY BIRD

Solitary Bird is devoted to introducing ideas to people outside of the academic world by creating fun, educational content for people at all walks of life. You can stimulate and expand your mind and meet other curious, open-minded people in an welcoming atmosphere. Solitary Bird is a place that allows for serendipity on the web. Headquartered in Falls Church, VA.

TECHNOLOGY

Solitary Bird uses the following resources: HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, Node.js, Express, Heroku and Travis CI
